Gold Miner ngumdlalo for Android ukuthi kukunika amandla okulawula i prospector elidala ngifuna igolide yini enye uyokwenza imali. Kuyinto umdlalo ikakhulu yakhelwe isikhathi, futhi udinga ukuqoqa imali eyanele ukuhlangabezana nemigomo ezingeni ukuze athuthuke ezingeni elilandelayo. Njengoba Android games hamba, Gold Miner uyinto ezizokhanga more imidlalo ezikhululekile kunokuba labo abakufunayo enkulu elandelayo umdlalo wabo mhlawumbe. With ihluzo agqamile umculo sihehe ngemuva Nokho, Gold Miner kungase kube umdlalo Arcade ukuthi ubulokhu elinde.

Indlela Esebenza: Wena uyonikezwa isingeniso esheshayo zendlela ukudlala umdlalo futhi ngempela, kukhona izinto ezimbalwa kuphela akhumbule ukuthi limnandi.

I uzipho ngeke iyahamba ohlangothini-kuya-side futhi uma badonsa esibukweni uzoba kufike phansi emgodini at engela wayeka it at.
